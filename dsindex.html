About Us

About Denshi

Founded during March 2021 by a young group of people based in Malaysia, Denshi™ is an e-commerce platform aimed towards the gaming community.

 

The name of the platform, Denshi originates from the Japanese translation of the term "electronic" (translates to "電子" in Kanji). The design of the online platform is highly inspired by the world's leading lifestyle brand for gamers, Razer™. The simplicity of the design of the website brings comforts to the eyes and welcomes everybody from any kind of background to use our website. We offer gaming products from various brands and they come with various price ranges as well. Cheap or expensive you can find them all in one place, truly a gamer's dream!

 

Denshi's services are now currently available in Malaysia only. Our goal is to expand our company overseas in the near future and connect with gamers all around the globe.
